Required Documents Checklist:

  • Proof of Registration (DTI, SEC, or CDA)
  • Proof of Legal Ownership or Possession
  • Owned:
    • Transfer Certificate of Title or Tax Declaration
  • Not Owned:
    • Contract of Lease
    • Memorandum of Agreement
    • or Notarized Consent of Property Owner
  • Picture with Signage
  • Fire Safety Inspection Certificate
  • Zoning Clearance
  • HOA Permit (if inside a subdivision))
  • Occupancy Permit/Engineering Clearance
  • CNC/ECC (if applicable)
  • Sanitary Permit
  • Barangay Clearance
  • Written Authority of Representative with Government Issued IDs (if applicable)

All documents must be clear, complete, and in PDF, JPG, or PNG format.

Our AI-powered system will scan your documents for authenticity and extract information automatically.
